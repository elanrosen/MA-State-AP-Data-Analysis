Why

There are many notable education ranking sites. They too attempt to provide a fair and hollistic ranking of high schools. However, they are two hollistic in this pursuit: ranking, evaluating, and analyzing on an entire student bodies' achievement. While these methods may provide a strong assesmenet of a school's performance, they suggest little about the quality of education for more advanced content.

By reviewing instead, the data of high performing students, we are able to derive predictions for a school's aptitude for producing top students, admissions to top schools, and other key factors in deciding an academic future.
